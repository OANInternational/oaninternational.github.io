We are glad to introduce a new way to **publish content** on the OAN
International website.

From now on, any team member can write a blog post directly in **Google Docs**,
export it to Markdown and publish it, with no technical knowledge required.

## What does this change enable?

- Writing posts in Google Docs, as always.
- Publishing in **Spanish and English**.
- Adding images, links and lists.
